计算距离放假还有多少天

 now <- Sys.time()
myTime <- strptime("2019-07-13 12:00:00", format = "%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S")
difftime(myTime,now)
## Time difference of 31.74783 days

计算距离放假还有多少小时

difftime(myTime,now,  units = "hours")
## Time difference of 761.948 hours

计算距离放假还有多少分钟

difftime( myTime,now, units = "mins")
## Time difference of 45716.88 mins

计算距离放假还有多少秒

difftime(myTime,now,  units = "secs")
## Time difference of 2743013 secs
